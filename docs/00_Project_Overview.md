\# Vendly Project Overview

Version: 1.0  
Status: Foundation Document

\---

\# 1\. Executive Summary

Vendly is a verification-first B2B marketplace designed to connect  
businesses with verified project opportunities and trusted service  
providers.

The platform enables Leads, Vendors, and Administrators to collaborate  
through a structured, transparent, and digital-first workflow for  
tenders and back-to-back projects.

Vendly prioritizes trust, efficiency, scalability, and usability while  
providing a modern enterprise SaaS experience.

\---

\# 2\. Vision

To become the preferred platform for discovering, publishing, and  
managing verified B2B opportunities across infrastructure,  
construction, industrial services, events, and related sectors.

\---

\# 3\. Mission

Vendly exists to:

\- Digitize fragmented procurement workflows  
\- Increase transparency between stakeholders  
\- Simplify opportunity discovery  
\- Reduce administrative overhead  
\- Encourage participation through verification and trust

\---

\# 4\. Core Principles

The product should always be:

\- Clean  
\- Professional  
\- Minimal  
\- Fast  
\- Secure  
\- Accessible  
\- Mobile-first  
\- Enterprise-ready

Every feature should reinforce these principles.

\---

\# 5\. Primary User Types

\#\# Visitor

An unauthenticated user exploring the platform.

Capabilities:  
\- Browse public pages  
\- Register  
\- Log in  
\- View legal information

\---

\#\# Lead

A business user searching for opportunities.

Capabilities:  
\- Maintain profile  
\- Browse vendors  
\- View opportunities  
\- Apply for projects  
\- Raise queries  
\- Track applications

\---

\#\# Vendor

A service provider participating in projects.

Capabilities:  
\- Register  
\- Maintain company information  
\- Complete verification  
\- Participate in opportunities

\---

\#\# Administrator

Platform operator responsible for governance.

Capabilities:  
\- Verify accounts  
\- Publish opportunities  
\- Manage disputes  
\- Respond to queries  
\- Configure settings  
\- Manage permissions

\---

\---

\# 7\. Project Categories

\#\# Tender

Projects with formal submission deadlines and structured documentation.

Common documents:  
\- BOQ  
\- RFP  
\- Drawings  
\- Technical specifications

\---

\#\# Back-to-Back

Projects awarded through subcontracting or direct allocation.

Common attributes:  
\- Award date  
\- Royalty percentage  
\- Win status  
\- Supporting files
